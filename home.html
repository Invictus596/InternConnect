About InternConnect

InternConnect is a web-based platform designed to connect students with multinational companies, providing a streamlined and user-friendly system for internship opportunities.

Key Features

College Registration

Colleges can register on the portal, giving students access to hundreds of internship opportunities.

Student Registration

Students can create profiles, browse internship options, and apply directly to companies.

Internship Listings

Multinational companies can post detailed internship requirements, including skills and qualifications.

Visibility of Requirements

Students can evaluate internship requirements to ensure they qualify before applying.

Direct Interaction

Students can interact directly with companies, simplifying the application process.

Our Goals

  • A centralized platform for students, colleges, and companies to connect efficiently.
  • Simplify the process of finding, evaluating, and applying for internships.
  • Enhance students' career building through industry experience.
